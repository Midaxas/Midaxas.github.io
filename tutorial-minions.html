ü§ñ What Are Minions?

Minions are NPCs that work for you automatically, generating resources and items even when you're offline!

Key Features

  • Always Active: Work 24/7 whether you're online or not
  • Resource Generation: Create items that you can harvest
  • Limited Slots: You start with 1 slot on your island
  • Tier System: Minions have Tiers I-XI (1-11)
  • Inventory Based: Items pile up in minion's inventory

How Minions Work

  1. Purchase minion egg from NPC shop or auction house
  2. Right-click to place minion on your island
  3. Minion generates items automatically
  4. Right-click minion to collect items (or use hoppers)
  5. Minion works even while you sleep!

‚öôÔ∏è Minion Upgrades & Enchantments

Upgrading Minion Tiers

Higher tiers = faster collection = more items = more income!

  • Tier I ‚Üí II: Costs 2x Tier I minion
  • Tier II ‚Üí III: Costs 2x Tier II minion
  • Each upgrade speeds up collection by ~10%
  • Tier XI (Max): Only for richest players

Optimal Upgrade Strategy

For New Players:

  1. Get 1 Tier I minion to start passive income
  2. Save up coins from other activities
  3. Upgrade to Tier II once you have enough
  4. Add new minions before upgrading
  5. Diversify: 5 T2 minions > 1 T5 minion

Minion Enchantments (Late Game)

  • Fuel: Burns items to generate more
  • Dispersor: Doubles minion output (insanely expensive!)
  • Compactor: Compresses items (saves space)
  • XP Boost: Gives you more XP

üöÄ Advanced Minion Strategies

1. Hopper Collection System

Use hoppers to automatically collect minion output:

  1. Place minion on island
  2. Place hopper under minion
  3. Connect hoppers in chain to storage barrel
  4. Minion items auto-collect into barrel
  5. No manual collection needed!

2. Compactor Minions

Use Compactor enchantment to save space:

  • Items compress to single stacks
  • More items fit in less space
  • Great for small islands
  • Cost: Several million per enchant

3. Fuel Minions Strategy

Power minions with fuel for 5x output:

  • Add coal/fuel to minion inventory
  • Minion uses fuel to work faster
  • Cost of fuel vs profit = decide if worth
  • Great for time-limited events

4. Dream Minion Setup (100m+ wealth)

20 Slots - Maximum Profit:

  • 8x Diamond Minion T11 (with Dispersor)
  • 5x Fishing Minion T11 (with Dispersor)
  • 4x Gold Minion T11
  • 3x Iron Minion T11

Daily Income: 500m+ coins

Cost: 5+ billion coins
