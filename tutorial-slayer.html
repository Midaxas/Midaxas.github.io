üéØ Slayer Basics

Slayers are solo/duo boss encounters. You summon a boss by using a slayer book / summoning item, then fight it.

How Slayers Work

  1. Get Slayer Summoning item (craft or buy from NPC)
  2. Right-click to summon the boss
  3. Combat the boss following its mechanics
  4. Defeat it and collect drops
  5. Gain Slayer XP to level up that slayer

üê∫ Sven Werewolf (Best for Beginners)

Sven is the easiest slayer and most profitable for new players. Perfect starter slayer!

Boss Mechanics

  • Attack Pattern: Charges at you every 3-4 seconds
  • Recovery State: Vulnerable after charge (2 seconds)
  • Healing: Does not heal itself
  • Minions: None spawned
  • Speed: Very fast movement

Combat Strategy

Step-by-Step:

  1. Summon Sven - Use Sven Bloodhound Egg (costs 100 coins)
  2. Dodge Charges - Run backward when Sven starts charging
  3. Counter-Attack - Hit Sven during recovery (right after charge ends)
  4. Repeat - Dodge, dodge, attack cycle
  5. Manage Health - Use healing potions when below 50%

Optimal Gear (Tier 1-2)

  • Sword: Iron or Diamond Sword
  • Armor: Gold or Emerald (25-30 defense)
  • Health: 100+ HP
  • Speed: On boots helps dodging

Optimal Gear (Tier 3-4)

  • Sword: AOTD or Cleaver (200+ damage)
  • Armor: Necrotic or Wise Armor
  • Health: 200+ HP
  • Strategy: Tank damage with healing from Necrotic

üï∑Ô∏è Tarantula Broodmother

The second slayer. Tarantula spawns minions and requires AOE damage to be efficient.

Boss Mechanics

  • Attack Pattern: Shoots projectiles in waves
  • Minions: Spawns 3-5 small spiders (weak)
  • Movement: Hovers and teleports
  • Phases: Gets faster/angrier as health drops
  • Difficulty: Medium - requires focus

Combat Strategy

  1. Clear Minions First: Use AOE weapon (cleaver preferred)
  2. Attack Boss: While avoiding projectiles
  3. Moving Target: Boss moves around - stay mobile
  4. Dodge Projectiles: Move side-to-side
  5. Rinse & Repeat: Kill minions, damage boss, heal

Recommended Gear

  • Weapon: Cleaver or Aotd (for AOE)
  • Armor: Necrotic (healing from AOE damage)
  • Stat Priority: Strength > Defense > Speed

  • HP: 50-100 hearts (tier dependent)
  • Requires: Skilled players with 100m+ gear

üë§ Enderman Slayer
