Frequently Asked Questions

Focus on completing the early quests, setting up basic minions, and building a sugar cane farm for passive income. Once you have ~500k coins, invest in better pickaxes and start exploring mining as a primary income source.

You should have at least 2-3 million coins and a good set of armor (Full Diamond minimum) before attempting dungeons. This ensures you can handle early dungeon runs and cover failure costs.

It depends on your playstyle and progress. Slaying is efficient for combat-focused players, fishing for those who like AFK, minions for passive income, and flipping for those interested in economics. Try different methods to find what suits you!

Use the Skyblock menu (/skyblock) to create or join a co-op. You can share island slots with up to 3 other players. Make sure to set up roles and permissions to keep the island organized.

Hypixel releases major updates roughly every 1-2 months, with smaller balance patches in between. Follow the official Hypixel forums and Twitter for the latest update news.
