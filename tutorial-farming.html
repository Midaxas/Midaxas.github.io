üå± Farming Basics

Farming is one of the most reliable and repeatable income sources in Skyblock. Unlike combat or mining, farming is completely predictable and scales with efficiency.

Required Materials

  • Hoe: Any hoe works, but higher tiers work faster
  • Seeds: Purchase from NPC shop (25 coins per seed)
  • Water: Create hydrated farmland - place water adjacent to dirt/grass
  • Land: Cultivate accessible farm area

Farming Mechanics

Growth Stages

Each crop goes through 4 growth stages before harvest. Display progress with /skyblock menu!

  • Stage 1: Seedling (1-25% mature)
  • Stage 2: Young plant (26-50% mature)
  • Stage 3: Mature plant (51-75% mature)
  • Stage 4: Ready for harvest (76-100% mature)

Growth Speed Factors

  • Farming skill level (higher = faster growth)
  • Crop type (different crops grow at different rates)
  • Organic matter/fertilizer (boosts growth by 10-50%)
  • Enchantments on hoe (Replenish, Green Thumb, etc.)

‚ö° Advanced Optimization Techniques

AFK Farming Setup

Create a setup where crops harvest automatically while you're offline!

  1. Place crops in organized rows
  2. Use hoppers under water channels to collect items
  3. Route hoppers to large storage barrel
  4. Equip hoe with Replenish enchantment
  5. Stand in one spot and let the farm work

Fertilizer System

Speed up growth by 10-50% with organic matter:

  • Create composters: 7 wooden slabs + 1 fence gate
  • Feed with plants: Sugarcane, wheat, leaves
  • Collect compost: Use right-click on composter
  • Apply to farmland: Right-click farmland with compost

Profit Maximization

  • Bulk Selling: Sell 64+ items at once for better prices
  • Time Your Sales: Avoid market saturation times
  • Quality Crops: Higher farming skill = higher quality items
  • Double Crops: Use enchantments to occasionally harvest 2x
