Combat Scaling

Damage formula: Base Weapon Damage + Strength Bonus √ó Modified by Crit Chance & Crit Damage

  • Every 5 points of Defense = 1 less damage per hit
  • Strength adds directly to damage per swing
  • Crit Chance: Up to 100% (soft cap at 50%)
  • Crit Damage: Typically 100-300% for end-game

Choose based on your playstyle:

  • Melee Fighters: Use swords or axes (AOTD best bang-for-buck)
  • Speed Runners: Use fast weapons with attack speed
  • Tanks: Focus on armor, any weapon with strength scaling
  • AOE Farmers: Use cleaver or explosive weapons

üè∞ Introduction to Dungeons

Dungeons are 5-player co-op instances featuring combat challenges, puzzles, and boss fights. This is where serious loot drops!

Requirements

  • Minimum Gear: Full Diamond armor + any sword
  • Recommended: Full Gold/Emerald armor + good weapon
  • Funds: 500k+ for deaths/repairs
  • Team: Find 4-5 other players

Dungeon Tiers

Tier Difficulty Recommended Gear Loot Quality
Floor 1 Beginner Diamond armor Common
Floor 2-3 Easy Gold armor Uncommon
Floor 4-5 Medium Emerald armor Rare
Floor 6-7 Hard Necrotic armor Epic
Floor 8+ Extreme Dungeon gear Legendary

Basic Dungeon Strategy

  1. Gather as a team - Don't split up
  2. Solve the puzzles - Each room has a mechanic
  3. Clear mobs efficiently - AOE weapons help
  4. Heal when needed - Use potions and food
  5. Boss fight: Learn attack patterns, use teamwork

üê∫ Slayer Boss Guide

Slayers are solo (or duo) boss battles. Choose one and master it!

Tarantula Broodmother Fast-paced 100 coins 1-5k XP

Sven Werewolf Strategy (Best for Beginners)

  1. Gear: Gold/Emerald armor + strong sword
  2. Attack Pattern: Sven charges at you every 3 seconds
  3. Strategy:
    • Back away from the charge
    • Hit him during recovery
    • Manage stamina/cooldowns
    • Use healing potions when needed
  4. Rewards: Werewolf XP (level up = unlock higher tiers)

Slayer Progression

Each slayer has 5 tiers. Higher tiers = harder bosses but better loot:

  • Tier 1: Introductory, low risk
  • Tier 2-3: Moderate difficulty
  • Tier 4: Challenging, good loot
  • Tier 5: Extreme difficulty, best loot rates
